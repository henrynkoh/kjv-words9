# KJV Words

KJV Bible verses by **topic/category** for **discussing and spreading words of truth**, depending on **audiences**, **situations**, and **level of intelligence and knowledge on Bible verses**.  
Format and style follow [kjv-faith-ai](https://github.com/henrynkoh/kjv-faith-ai).

> "Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." — 2 Timothy 2:15 KJV

## Purpose

- **Topics/categories** — Verses grouped by theme (Creation & Redemption, Faith & Holy Spirit, Tradition & Law, etc.).
- **Audience** — Use when speaking to believers, seekers, new believers, mature, mixed, or children.
- **Situation** — Use for evangelism, teaching, comfort, correction, devotion, worship, or counsel.
- **Knowledge level** — Fits any, beginner, moderate, or deeper Bible knowledge.

Each verse includes a Korean summary (흠정역 한글성경전서 style). Pick the right verse for the right person and moment.

## Topics

| Topic | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Creation & Redemption | Creation through Him, light vs darkness, one blood, judgment, righteousness, the Lamb |
| Faith, Belief & Holy Spirit | Dying in sin, miracles for belief, Comforter, work of God |
| Tradition, Law & True Worship | God's will vs tradition, lips vs heart, vain worship, law and justification |
| Religious Leaders & Hypocrisy | Whited sepulchres, shutting the kingdom, purpose of miracles |
| Israel, Gentiles & Gospel | Turning to the Gentiles, Jerusalem Council, apostles, God's wisdom |
| Study & Christian Living | Rightly dividing, Bereans, persecution, maturity, Adam vs Christ |
| Kingdom & New Creation | Thy will be done, new heaven and earth, no temple, no more pain |
| Daily Walk & Provision | Narrow way, seek first the kingdom, thanksgiving, walk in the Spirit |

## Run

```bash
npm run dev
```
